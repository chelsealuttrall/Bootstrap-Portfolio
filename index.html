About Me

Hi, there! I'm Chelsea. I'm currently a prosthetist/orthotist, and I work with kiddos who need my help to walk and move. I am married to my favorite person in the world, and we have two giant dogs and a cat who thinks he is a dog. We live in Colorado Springs, in our dream home in the mountains. I hope we never have to move.

I'm a mountain biker and retired adventure racer. I have some crazy outdoor stories from my days of racing in the wilderness for days on end. They were good (and sometimes scary) times, but now I prefer the simplicity of days spent mountain biking or mountaineering with friends. It feeds my soul!

For additional fun and skill-building, I work in a race car shop. I help the owner build his race projects, but I am also in the midst of converting my motorcycle to electric. I want my motorcycle to be my daily commuter and errand-runner vehicle. Fun stuff!

Feel free to check out my projects or drop me a line!
